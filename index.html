Captain Doge Mission

Disrupting the entertainment industry with the power of blockchain.

Vision Statement

At Captain Doge we see a future where the rise of web 3.0 will propel crypto to mainstream adoption.

One of the challenges we see on this journey to adoption is a lack of understanding of blockchain technology and its many wonderful applications.

Entertainment is without a doubt the most powerful medium in existence to reach the masses.

By bringing to life the most iconic worlds, stories & characters of web 3.0 through comics, games, music & animation, Captain Doge will be the vessel through which young & old can learn about cryptocurrency, web 3.0, NFTs & the Metaverse.

With an experienced team with backgrounds in blockchain research, digital marketing, finance, art & design, Captain Doge is building THE superhero franchise of web 3.0.

We invite you on a hero’s journey to defend the Doge Metaverse…

Captain Doge Core Goals & Values

Entertain

Entertainment is the best medium to capture attention. Tech-focused projects are building the foundation of a decentralized web 3.0, yet they often lack the relatability to reach the masses. Captain Doge aims to entertain first, educate about the benefits of decentralized technologies second. AAA quality Comics, Games & Animations created with passion, dedication and a love for storytelling will lay the foundation for the Captain Doge project.

Educate

We will spark interest in blockchain technology through entertainment. We will present the benefits of decentralization and educate about practical applications of web 3.0 as it evolves. Through an open community culture we encourage exchange of experience & ideas in our channels. Educational Videos, FAQ’s & Comics and a variety of other resources will make learning about blockchain technology a great experience.

Empower

We believe empowering the individual is the ultimate goal of decentralization. Through entertainment and education Captain Doge is building the foundation for a new generation of web 3.0 users to empower themselves and others through the use of blockchain technology.

P2E Game

Gamers spend over $125B on in-game purchases in 2021. This money goes directly into the game developer’s pockets, and players cannot sell their purchases to other players.

Blockchain & NFT’s are on their way to disrupt this market and make gaming fair again by giving players more control over their in-game assets and purchases.

P2E gaming is the future, but is not without challenges.

P2E games are graphically underwhelming, the quality is not competitive with the mainstream gaming market.

Using Unreal Engine 5, Captain Doge is developing a game with great graphics that can compete with mainstream gaming developers.

P2E gaming is about to go to another level.
